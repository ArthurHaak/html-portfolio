Best movies of all time according to Arthur Haak

The best of the best.

Star Wars Episode 3: Revenge of the Sith

The conclusion of the prequel trilogy tells the fall of the Republic and rise of the Galatic Empire and Darth Vader. Good fight scenes, memorable dialogue and loveable characters mixed in a wonderful universe.

Coco

A beautiful story about the passing and memory of loved ones, be ready to drop a lot of tears and feel many feelings.

Super Mario Bros: The Movie

Very simple plot with amazing voice acting behind our beloved characters from the videogames. Absolute perfection for the fans of the plumber with a moustache with many references and a superb soudtrack
